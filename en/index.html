Juvenile Criminal Law

The distinguishing feature of juvenile criminal law is its focus on education rather than punishment. If your child is in the sights of the justice system, I will support you with my expertise to find the best possible solution. My goal is to preserve the future prospects of young people and to create a sustainable perspective through strategic defense. I will guide you and your family with confidence through this often burdensome process.

Traffic Law

The consequences in traffic law are often underestimated, yet they directly affect your mobility. If you are facing a fine, a driving ban, or have been involved in an accident, I will support you with professional expertise.
