Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about initial contact, acute criminal law situations, juvenile criminal law, and traffic law.

Acute Criminal Law Situations

What should I do in case of an arrest, search, or seizure?

In acute criminal law situations, quick and level-headed action is important. I offer an emergency number that you can reach outside of regular business hours as well. Early legal support can be crucial, especially in cases of arrest, search, or seizure.

Why is early case file review so important in criminal law?

Early case file review and a carefully developed strategy are often crucial for the further course of proceedings. Knowing the status of the investigation early on allows the defense to be more targeted. I make every effort to influence the proceedings in your favor from the very beginning.

Juvenile Criminal Law and Traffic Law

Do you also support parents and families in juvenile criminal law?

Yes. In juvenile criminal law, the focus is not only on the legal situation but also on the future prospects of the young people involved. I guide juveniles and their families with confidence through an often burdensome process and advocate for the best possible outcome.

How exactly can you help with traffic law?

I support you in traffic law matters, particularly with fine proceedings, driving bans, and accidents. This includes all communication with insurance companies and authorities, as well as enforcing your claims and defending against sanctions. My goal is to keep you mobile.

Initial Contact and Documents

It is helpful to bring all documents related to your case – such as summons, letters from the police, public prosecutor's office, court, authorities, or insurance companies. You can also download the client questionnaire as a PDF and fill it out in advance to prepare our first meeting efficiently.

What is the client questionnaire useful for?

The client questionnaire helps you organize important information and prepare our first meeting efficiently. This facilitates communication and enables me to provide a faster, more informed initial assessment of your situation.
